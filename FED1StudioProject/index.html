About Us

Education for Tomorrow focuses on fulfilling the needs for both creative license and accurate information displayed by our multifaceted community. Our dedicated faculty have more than 100 years of combined experience in the worlds of print and digital publishing. In-house experts are also highly valued professionals in the world of print. This allows us to keep up to date with publishing trends.

Why Choose Us

Digital publishing has opened the publishing world to millions of people around the world. This means more competition and a plethora of options available to publishers. Considerations like document type and file extension compatibility must be considered carefully to ensure the reach of your product is broad and fair. Our experts can also help you make the conversion from traditional print to digital platforms chosen based on your specific case. Learn about cutting edge technologies that have already stated to outpace traditional paper prints and what you can do to keep up and push the envelope yourself.

Is printed media becoming extinct? Without a doubt the market for traditional print media has been visibly shrinking. The speed with which digital media can be updated and distributed creates incredible obstacles to the traditional publishing house. Don’t despair! Let us show you the options that are available int the print world and how new technologies like reader tablets will give you the ability to use those traditional print layouts to display your information while helping your publishing house make the transition to digital!
